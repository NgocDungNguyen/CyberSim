Virtual SOC & Incident Response Simulator

What is a Security Operations Center (SOC)?

A Security Operations Center (SOC) is a centralized unit that monitors, detects, and responds to cybersecurity threats in real-time. SOC teams are responsible for ensuring the security of an organization's digital assets.

This simulation combines a virtual SOC environment with an incident response simulator to demonstrate how security professionals handle cyber threats.

Step 2: Incident Response

Follow the steps below to respond to the incident:

  1. Identify the source of the threat.
  2. Contain the threat to prevent further damage.
  3. Eradicate the threat by removing malicious files or blocking attackers.
  4. Recover affected systems and restore normal operations.
  5. Conduct a post-incident analysis to improve future defenses.
