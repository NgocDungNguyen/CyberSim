About CyberSec Learning Hub

Our Mission

CyberSec Learning Hub is an educational project created by Nguyen Ngoc Dung (June Ng) with the mission to empower individuals with essential cybersecurity knowledge. Our goal is to make cybersecurity education accessible, engaging, and effective for students and professionals alike.

Through this interactive learning platform, we aim to demystify cybersecurity concepts, raise awareness about emerging threats, and provide practical strategies for protection in the digital age.

About the Creator

Nguyen Ngoc Dung (June Ng)

June Ng is a passionate cybersecurity enthusiast and student at RMIT University in Hanoi. With a keen interest in making cybersecurity education more accessible, June created CyberSec Learning Hub as an educational project to help others learn about cyber threats and protection strategies.

While currently developed as an individual project, June hopes to collaborate with other experts and enthusiasts in the future to expand the platform's content and reach. The ultimate goal is to see CyberSec Learning Hub implemented in real-life educational settings and schools, contributing to a more cyber-aware society.
